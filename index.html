Road map

There are a couple of anime NFTs out there on the blockchain today and even though we have joined the lot, we think of ourselves as creating anime NFT that standout. We basically seek to achieve this by following the very simple logic behind such NFT’s, ensuring anime lovers have a thrilling experience and enjoy such pictures.

This just goes to say that we are developing a community where the feedback of members of the community is what essentially keeps the community running. We seek to pay attention to the needs and desires of our members and ensure optimal experience and also the ability to provide the ideas in mass.

Conception
We are here to provide you with unforgettable emotions that you can have in our chats, and find people that are interested in the same as you. Our 2d collection provides full control of choice on the next stages of development. Such as a 3d collection that is in progress right now.
Our 3d Collection
Our 3d anime collection will be introduced to the metaverse in April. All holders of 2d collection will get an airdrop of one if they hold 2 or more. Each 2 will surely provide you an airdrop of 3d collection.
Burning system
Connecting the dots of our technical expertise with the ideas and comments of members of our community, we decide to make a burning system that will provide full sold out regardless of the number of sold.
Democracy
We seek to offer our best to all members of our community and majorly those who are holders in our community. We appreciate inputs from everyone but when it comes to putting it to a vote, the more nfts you have or the more valuable they are, the greater the punch your vote parks.
